What are AI Agents?

AI agents are like digital employees, capable of handling tasks autonomously to drive business growth. Examples include AI phone agents that can handle call inquiries, website chatbots for customer support, and email agents for sales and support. These agents interact with stakeholders, providing efficient and scalable solutions to enhance business operations.
